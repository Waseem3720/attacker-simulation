Tactics: Ransomware, phishing, card skimming

Target: Businesses, banks, individuals

Impact: Financial loss, stolen data

Artifacts

  • Fake ransom note mockup
  • Phishing email screenshot
  • Brag card image

Timeline

  1. Recon: Identify vulnerable systems
  2. Delivery/Access: Deploy ransomware or phishing

Impact: Loss of sensitive data, operational disruption
